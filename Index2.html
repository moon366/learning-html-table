Aayusa Neupane

Nature has always been my escape and my teacher. Whether it’s the gentle rustling of leaves in the wind or the warm glow of the setting sun, I find peace in every part of the natural world. Walking in a forest or sitting quietly near a river helps me slow down and appreciate life’s simple beauty. Spending time in nature reminds me to breathe deeply, live mindfully, and respect the planet we call home. It teaches me patience, balance, and the value of silence. In nature, I feel connected—not just to the Earth, but to myself. Nature and I share a quiet friendship. It’s where I feel most alive.
